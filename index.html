Welcome to the sports club of historical fencing "Black Company". We are the first and only HEMA fencing club in Dnipro. Together with us you can plunge into the magical world of history of the 16-18 centuries, develop your physical skills, attend numerous tournaments and festivals, as well as spend time in a cheerful company of like-minded people.

What are we doing?

Historical European martial arts (HEMA) are martial arts of European origin, particularly using arts formerly practised, but having since died out or evolved into very different forms. This direction has been actively developing in Ukraine for the past 15 years. Every year, more than 10 different events are held where you can exchange experiences with successful fencers and just have a good time.
